## How to find more like these

1. **Google Maps grid searches by vertical + town.** Search "[vertical] near Hockessin DE / Greenville DE / Newark DE / Cherry Hill NJ" and look for thin/missing website links, no "Book Online" button, few photos, or Facebook-only. The *absence* of a site is the strongest buy signal.
2. **Chamber of commerce directories.** Pull member lists (New Castle County Chamber, Greater Hockessin Area Development Association, Delaware State Chamber, Cherry Hill Regional Chamber) and visit each site — the dated ones are pre-qualified prospects already paying for visibility.
3. **Yelp / YellowPages / Angi "no website" listings.** Browse a category and click into listings with a phone but no website link — those are Launch-package leads handed to you.
4. **Mobile-fail audits.** Open candidate sites on your phone: not responsive, no click-to-call, >3s load, or a copyright year of 2019 or earlier = a concrete gap to lead outreach with (screenshot it).
5. **Local networking + BNI/referral groups.** Service-business owners cross-refer heavily; one happy client becomes a referral engine.
6. **"New owner / new location" signals.** New franchisees, second locations (e.g., Cacia's Haddonfield), and recent LLC formations (DE Division of Corporations) are actively spending and most receptive. Set a Google Alert for "now open" + your towns.
